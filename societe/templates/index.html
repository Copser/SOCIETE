WORK WITH US

Erasmus Haus provides furnished housing for the New York City
international community of students, interns, and young professionals.

Moving to New York will become one of the greatest
experiences of your life, but there will be various barriers
you will have to face during the moving process.
The most common one is finding a place to live.
It can be challenging to find an apartment in the huge city
like New York. We therefore offer a veriety of apartments
within differente price ranges all over New York.
We assure you that
we will find you something that matches your requiremnets.
Linette moved to New York as a Swedish exchange
student. After she arrived to the city, she spent three
weeks finding an apartment. Before she could move in to
her apartment, she had to pay a broker fee equivalent
to three mounths of rent on top of a huge security deposit.
After that, she paid a full year's rent upfront.
