 MISSION AND HISTORY

Originally started in 2009, Literati Group Housing began bacause of the lack of affordable, furnished housing for international community in New York City. Our goal is to offer housing accommodations for motivated, qualified, educated and trained individuals that strive to be at the top of their respective crafts. By partnering with local universities, internship programs, and specialty schools, there is no place like Literati Group Housing. We foster micro communities in our residences where residents serce multiple functions as roommate, friend, and city ambassador. Literati Group Housing strives to end the plight of misleading advertisment targeted to the international community and build our company with good service, ethics and a rigid selection criteria to residents partners and employees alike. There is no place like home and there is no place like Literati.
