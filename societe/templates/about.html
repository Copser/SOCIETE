WE ARE ERASMUS

 MISSION AND HISTORY

 

Originally started in 2009, Erasmus Haus began because of the lack of affordable,
furnished housing for internation community in New York City. Our goal is to offer housing
accommodations for motivated, qualified, educated and trained individuals that strive to be at
the top of their respective crafts. By partnering with local universities, internship programs, and
speciality schools, there is no place like Erasmus Haus. We foster micro communities in
our residences where residents serce multiple functions as roommate, friend, and city
ambassador. Erasmus Haus strives to end the plight of misleading advertisement targeted
to the internationl community and build our company with good service, ethics and a rigid
selection criteria to residents partners and employees alike. There is no place like home and
there is no place like Erasmus Haus.
